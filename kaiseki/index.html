About

Kaiseki is a sustainable packaging design project focused on delivering a refined consumer experience through material innovation and strategic visual communication. I led the structural development of the package, researching eco-friendly materials and prototyping a cradle-to-cradle solution supported by CAD modeling and photorealistic renderings.

In parallel, I directed the graphic identity, creating a bold and clean visual system that communicates freshness, quality, and environmental responsibility while aligning with premium market expectations.
