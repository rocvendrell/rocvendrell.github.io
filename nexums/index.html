About

Nexums is a large-scale light installation created for the LLUM BCN Festival, conceived as an immersive, site-specific experience that explores rhythm, form, and spatial perception through light. Developed collaboratively, the project transforms the urban space into a passage of glow and movement.

As lead designer, I guided both the creative and technical development, from site analysis and concept definition to CAD modeling, layout testing, and production of assembly plans. I coordinated LED distribution, managed materials and logistics, and led the on-site installation to ensure a cohesive final result.
