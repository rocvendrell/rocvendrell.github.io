About

HUMAN is a unisex fragrance project blending sustainable product design, material efficiency, and minimalist visual identity to express authenticity and modern luxury.

I developed the product and packaging design from concept to detail, working on form, material selection, and structural definition to balance symbolism, material efficiency, and sustainability. I also defined the visual system ensuring coherence between the object, its packaging, and the message of authenticity at the core of the project.
