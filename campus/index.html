About

Redesign of the Universitat de Barcelona's virtual campus, aimed at improving clarity, comfort, and overall usability. The project rethinks the platform with a simpler structure, reduced complexity, and a more contemporary visual language.

My work focused on information architecture, user flows, and wireframing, alongside the definition of a clean and modern visual system. Together, these elements shape a clearer navigation experience and a cohesive interface designed to make everyday academic tasks more intuitive and accessible.
