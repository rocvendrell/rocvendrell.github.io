About

Arxiu de Somnis is an ephemeral installation created for the A Cel Obert festival, transforming urban space into a shared archive of written dreams. Hundreds of suspended papers invite visitors to walk through the installation and actively contribute by adding their own dreams.

The project explores collective memory and participation, giving physical form to the intangible and turning the public space into a place for reflection, storytelling, and shared imagination.
