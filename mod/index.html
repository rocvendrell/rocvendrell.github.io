About

Museum of Open Design is a conceptual digital project that explores how a museum of design could exist in a fully virtual environment. The project includes the design of a website and mobile app, both developed with a modern neumorphic visual language.

I developed the information structure, navigation logic, and interface components, translating complex content into a clear and accessible digital experience. The project highlights skills in layout design, interaction definition, and system consistency across web and mobile environments.
