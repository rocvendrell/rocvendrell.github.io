About

Changsha is a versatile auxiliary furniture piece designed for office environments, conceived as a dynamic storage solution that adapts to different workspaces. Its simple form and stainless steel sheet construction ensure durability, ease of production, and recyclability.

The design emphasizes functionality and mobility, combining three offset trays for organized storage with wheels and a foldable handle that allow the piece to move easily between work areas, supporting flexible and efficient office use while maintaining a clean industrial aesthetic.
